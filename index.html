Why Equestrian Vendor Events Matter

In-person equestrian vendor events offer something online shopping cannot — the ability to try a saddle before buying, feel the quality of a bridle, assess the fit of a boot, or see a blanket in person before committing. Major equestrian vendor events at show venues and barn sale days generate significant buyer activity from riders who are already engaged in the sport and actively shopping for equipment. Elite Equine Marketplace helps riders find these events near their location and helps vendors reach the right audience for their products and sales.
